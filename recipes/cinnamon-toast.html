Air Fryer Cinnamon Toast

Cinnamon toast is made with just four ingredients: bread, butter, sugar, and cinnamon. I’ve discovered that the trick to cinnamon toast perfection is making sweetened cinnamon butter with a few drops of vanilla. CAN’T I JUST USE MY TOASTER? Unfortunately, you cannot achieve this heightened level of yum by simply toasting your bread, buttering it, and sprinkling it with cinnamon and sugar. You’ll only have flavor on the top of your toast, not throughout, and while it will be good, it won’t be BEYOND. And don’t we all need a little BEYOND in our lives?

Ingredients

  • 1 sliced bread
  • 2 tbsps salted butter
  • 4 drops vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 350°F. Add room temperature butter, vanilla, sugar, and cinnamon to a small bowl.
  2. Mix until incorporated.
  3. Spread half of the butter on one side of your slice of bread. Make sure to cover the slice from corner to corner.
  4. Air fry for five minutes.
  5. Take the toast out and spread the remaining butter on the other side, again corner to corner.
  6. Air fry for five minutes.
